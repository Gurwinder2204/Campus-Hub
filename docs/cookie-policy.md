# Cookie Policy — Campus Study Hub

_Last updated: March 2026_

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help maintain your session and preferences.

## Cookies We Use

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `JSESSIONID` | Session management (login state) | Browser session |
| `XSRF-TOKEN` | CSRF protection | Browser session |

## Third-Party Cookies

We use **Bootstrap** and **Bootstrap Icons** from CDN (jsdelivr.net). These CDNs may set their own cookies. We do not control third-party cookies.

## Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that disabling session cookies will prevent you from logging in.

## No Tracking Cookies

We do **not** use advertising, retargeting, or social media tracking cookies.
