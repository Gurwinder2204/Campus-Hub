# Privacy Policy — Campus Study Hub

_Last updated: March 2026_

## What Data We Collect

| Data | Purpose | Retention |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Email & password | Authentication | Until account deletion |
| Uploaded notes/papers | Academic resource sharing | Until deleted by uploader or admin |
| Booking metadata | Room reservation tracking | 1 year |
| Analytics events | Usage metrics & improvement | 90 days |
| Application logs | Debugging & security | 30 days |

## How We Use Your Data

- **Authentication**: Verify your identity and secure your account.
- **Content delivery**: Serve notes, papers, and videos relevant to your courses.
- **Booking management**: Process and track room reservation requests.
- **Analytics**: Understand usage patterns to improve features (aggregated, not individual).

## What We Don't Do

- ❌ We **never sell** your data to third parties.
- ❌ We **never share** personal information with advertisers.
- ❌ We **never use** your data for non-educational purposes.

## Camera & Notification Permissions

Some features (photo upload, AR tour) request camera access. Notifications require your explicit opt-in. You can revoke these at any time in your device settings.

## Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, contact the administrator at the email provided in your campus deployment. All associated data (uploads, bookings, analytics) will be removed within 14 business days.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be announced on the platform dashboard.
